Keeping track which electric car has what range is confusing. This is an attempt to assemble a list of the capacities and ranges for some vehicles of interest. Not complete. Not definitive. And surely not correct. Also note that different trim levels have different batteries capacities. In general, battery capacity has been increasing over the years (27kWh for pre-2017 models to 60kWh in 2020).

If we take the claimed battery capacity and divide by the claimed range, it seems we should expect at least 5km/kWh.
